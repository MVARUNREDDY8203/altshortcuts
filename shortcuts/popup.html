Alt Shortcuts
Alt + S Jump to Search
Alt + W Jump to Write/Compose
Alt + R Open a new tab to the Right
Alt + U Scroll to the top of the page
ALT + V Scroll to the bottom of the page
Alt + T Switch to last used tab
ALT + G Open Google Homepage and close all other tabs
ALT + D Duplicate tab
ALT + M Mute site
Alt + P Pin / Unpin tab
